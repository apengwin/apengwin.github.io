Autonomous Guitar

A self-playing voice-controlled guitar we hacked together during dead week 2016.

The strings and frets are plucked and pressed by arrays of solenoids carefully placed over the neck and sound hole of the guitar. We noted that notes corresponding to frets further down the necktend to be out of tune, though this could be remedied by implementing some pulse width modulation over the GPIO pin.

You can check out the source on github.
